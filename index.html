About Jasmin

Hi there! I'm a recent graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I studied Cartography/GIS in the Geography department and Television/Film in the Communication Arts department. While this combination of majors might seem unlikely, both programs have enriched my passion for visual storytelling.

Whether embarking on Cartography/GIS or Film/Television/Media pursuits, I am particularly intersted in issues relating to race, representation, urban geography, human geography, and the environment.

If you click on the portfolio tab on the navigation bar above, you'll find some of my previous classwork. Feel free to take a look around!
