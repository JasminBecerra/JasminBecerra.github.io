About Jasmin

Hi! I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Cartography/GIS in the department of Geography, and Film/Television in the Communication Arts. While this combination of majors may seem unrelated, what unites my fields of study is a core in storytelling. As far as Cartography goes, I'm interested in mapping urban human geography, and issues related to race and representation of marginalized groups!

I am currently working on an immigration-themed map that will be part digitally-made and part embroidered. Once my project is complete, I will include a blog post about it here-- so check back soon!
