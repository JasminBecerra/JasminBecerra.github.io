Below is my first attempt at customizing a tileset using Mapbox Studio Classic for a Graphic Design in Cartography course I took at UW-Madison in the Fall of 2016.
The assignment was to recreate the aesthetic of a source of our choice. My gut reactions were Maggie Taylor and Maurice Sendak's Where the Wild Things Are. I eventually chose the later (but I'll definitely be taking a stab at recreating Maggie Taylor's photographic style into a tileset in the future!). Because Mapbox tools have periodically changed and shifted what one can and cannot do, for this particular assignment, I had to map points of interest related to Sendak's work: places where his work has been exhbited, and even an OPERA based on Where the Wild Things Are!
