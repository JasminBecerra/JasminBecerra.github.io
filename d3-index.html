Harship Index by Chicago Neighborhoods

Below is a map-based visualization of socioeconomic indicators in Chicago from 2008 to 2012 collected by the City of Chicago in 2014. Each neighborhood on the map is linked to a corresponding bar in the chart to the right. You can change the mapped attribute by using the dropdown menu. This was made for my Interactive Cartography & Visualization course (Spring 2017), and it was my first time using d3.

To explore the map, please select an attribtue of interest!
