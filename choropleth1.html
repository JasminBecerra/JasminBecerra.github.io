I made the map below in my Intro to Cartography course during the Fall of 2015. This is one of the first maps I ever made!

For this project, I wanted to make a map of Latinos in higher education. As a Mexican-American and first-generation college student, I often think about how marginalized groups are represented in higher education. While the trends found in the map below are predictable (more degree-holding Latinos in urban coutnies) I think this map sheds light on the very sparse representation of Latinos in higher education, and might even help visualize the impact of education inequities.
Data was aquired from the US Census and American Community Survey. Map was created and processed thorugh ArcMap.
