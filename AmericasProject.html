Jasmin Becerra

Cartographer

This is a reference map of North, Central, and South America (plus Greenland, Iceland, and parts of Europe) and it is one of my earliest cartographic works.

This map was inspired largely by the episode of The West Wing (Season 2, Episode 19 to be exact)when a group of cartographers present C.J Cregg and Josh Lyman(Allison Janney's and Bradley Whitford's characters) with the inaccuracies of map projections-- they conclude with a "south-up" map of the world that leaves the White House personnel tilting their heads.

With that in mind, I thought it would be neat to make a south-up map of the Americas, with a hint of antique/vintage style.
